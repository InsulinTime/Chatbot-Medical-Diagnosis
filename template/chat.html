Emergency Assistance

Seek immediate care for:

  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding
  • Sudden weakness or numbness
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Severe allergic reactions
  • Severe pain anywhere on your body
  • Suicidal thoughts
